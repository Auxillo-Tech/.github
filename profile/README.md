# Auxillo

**Infrastructure intelligence for operators who need control, clarity, and speed.**

Auxillo builds security-first systems for managing real infrastructure: remote operations, diagnostics, automation, and AI-assisted command workflows.

We focus on practical tools that help technical teams understand what is happening, act safely, and keep control of their own systems.

## What Auxillo builds

- **Remote operations tooling** for managing Linux servers and infrastructure from one operator workstation
- **Security and diagnostics utilities** for investigating domains, networks, mail systems, certificates, and exposed services
- **AI-assisted troubleshooting systems** that help isolate failures, reason from evidence, and support safer remediation
- **Automation workflows** for deployment, monitoring, release handling, and operational repeatability
- **Self-hosted-first infrastructure** designed to reduce unnecessary dependence on third-party platforms

## Principles

- Evidence before assumption
- Security before convenience
- Local and self-hosted where practical
- Operator control over black-box abstraction
- Clear systems over fragile magic

## Status

Auxillo is under active development.

Most production systems are private while public-facing projects are prepared, hardened, documented, and released intentionally.
