# Security Policy

Auxillo takes security seriously.

If you believe you have found a vulnerability in an Auxillo system, please do not open a public issue with sensitive details.

## Reporting

Report security issues through Auxillo's public contact path:

- Website: [auxillo.tech](https://auxillo.tech)

A dedicated security mailbox may be published here once it is active.

Please include:

- affected system or URL
- vulnerability summary
- reproduction steps
- potential impact
- relevant logs, screenshots, or request/response examples

## Scope

Only systems owned and operated by Auxillo are in scope.

Do not perform destructive testing, denial-of-service testing, credential attacks, social engineering, physical attacks, or testing against third-party systems.

## Response

Valid reports are reviewed and prioritized based on severity, exploitability, affected assets, and business impact.
